‚ú® How Logo Overlay Works

The logo overlay feature automatically clears modules in the center of the QR code and embeds your logo image using SVG's <image> tag. By using Error Correction Level H (30% recovery), the QR code remains fully scannable even with up to 30% of the data obscured by the logo. The implementation supports logo sizes from 10% to 30% of the QR code dimensions.

Technical Implementation: The logo is rendered with a rounded background rectangle and the image is embedded with preserveAspectRatio="xMidYMid meet" to maintain proper scaling.
